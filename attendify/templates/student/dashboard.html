Procedure for Marking Attendance

Follow these simple steps to successfully mark your class attendance using Attendify.

Step 1: Login

Use your registered student account to log in to the Attendify portal securely.

Step 2: Access Attendance

Once logged in, click on the “Mark Attendance” tab to open your attendance page.

Step 3: Confirm Location

Ensure you are within the classroom or the allowed attendance zone before marking.

Step 4: Mark Attendance

Click the “Mark Attendance” button to submit your attendance instantly.

Step 5: Verify Status

After submission, confirm that your attendance has been successfully recorded.

Step 6: Track Records

You can view your attendance history anytime from your student dashboard.
